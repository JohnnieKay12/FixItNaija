About FixItNaija

FixItNaija connects Nigerians with verified and trusted artisans, quickly, safely, and conveniently. Whether you're in Lagos, Abuja, or beyond, we're your go-to platform for reliable service professionals.

Our Mission

Our mission is to empower local artisans by giving them visibility and providing customers with quick, secure, and easy access to professional services.

Our Story

Founded in Lagos by a team passionate about solving local problems with tech, FixItNaija was built to eliminate the struggle of finding skilled workers. With growing trust and thousands of users, we’re proud to be part of Nigeria’s digital transformation.
